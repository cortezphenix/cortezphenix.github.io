How My Relationship with Books Began

25th of September, 2020

In this post, I will be writing briefly about how I began my journey with books and how books have changed my life.

Pictured above, there is a book called Satch & Me, written by Dan Gutman. It was the first book that I read, outside of the compulsory context of school. My third grade teacher lent it to me, and I read it in one day. Reading that book, I realized the beauty and power of words. Reading books, I transported myself to other worlds. Reading books became a form of escapism for Young Me. After reading that book, I read the rest of the series. I began devouring all the fiction I could, finishing the series and moving on to others, like Percy Jackson and Harry Potter. I also enjoyed reading books about life in Ancient Greece, Ancient Rome, and Medieval Europe.

That initial experience with fiction, eventually led to me exploring nonfiction. To cut a long story short, without my teacher introducing me to books, I would have never become the person I am today, with the interests I have. For that, I am really grateful. I am also grateful for the public library system and my mother, who graciously transported Young Me to libraries.

by Cortez Phenix
