Introduction

23rd of September, 2020

Yo! Welcome to my first blog post. I am excited to begin this. There will be little-to-no traffic on this site, but that is OK. Really, the main purpose I have for this site is to have a spot to dump my thoughts, opinions, and experiences, all made accessible to the public. My goal is to write content, then be able to access it anywhere. I am used to talking to myself, so the lack of traffic will not be a problem. :-)

That's all, folks. Let's get to writing.
